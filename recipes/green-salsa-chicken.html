Sweet Green Salsa Chicken Recipe

Green salsa chicken

This green salsa chicken recipe is super quick and easy. it can be done in the pressure cooker in less than half an hour

Ingredients

  • 3-4 tomatoes
  • 3 jalapenos/1 serrano pepper (feel free to use 2 jalapenos/2 serranos for spicier salsa)
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp knorr suiza buillon
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 handful cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat pan to 6/7 and begin charring the tomatoes and peppers
  2. Peel the garlic gloves, and once you are ready, brown the sides. (Yes, char the peppers and tomatoes completely, but only brown the garlic)
  3. In a blender, throw in water, vinegar, buillon, cilantro
  4. Once the tomatoes and peppers have charred on all sides, and you have browned garlic, throw them into the blender
  5. Blend and enjoy
